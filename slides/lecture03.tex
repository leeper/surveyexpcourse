\frametitle{Share your TESS Examples}

In groups of 4--5, share your TESS examples
\begin{itemize}
\item What was the researcher's question?
\item How did they test it experimentally?
\item What was interesting or surprising about the designs?
\end{itemize}

Take about 7--8 minutes.

}



\frame{

\frametitle{Groupwork}

In \textbf{new} groups of 4--5, develop a survey experiment
\begin{itemize}
\item What's your research question?
\item What is/are your hypotheses?
\item What's the outcome? How will you measure it?
\item How will you operationalize your causal variable?
\item Also think about: sampling, etc.
\end{itemize}

Presentations 14:45--15:45
